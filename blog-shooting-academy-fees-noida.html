How Much Does It Cost to Learn Shooting in India? (2026 Edition)

By Noida Shooting Hub Editorial Last Updated Feb 19, 2026 Category Beginner's Guide

Shooting has evolved from a niche hobby into one of India’s most successful competitive sports. With world-class facilities now available across the NCR, joining a shooting academy in Noida is more accessible than ever. But what is the financial commitment required to go from a beginner to a pro?

"Investing in shooting sports is an investment in discipline and focus. While the initial equipment costs can be high, the monthly training fees in India remain among the most competitive globally."

1. Initial Training & Membership Fees

For most beginners, the journey starts with a basic foundation course. Academies in Noida typically provide all necessary equipment during this phase.

  • Foundation Course (10-15 Days): ₹2,500 – ₹7,500
  • Monthly Coaching Fees: ₹6,000 – ₹9,000 (Includes weapon rental)
  • One-time Registration: Approx. ₹10,000

2. Cost Breakdown Table

Expense Type Estimated Cost (INR) Frequency
Academy Membership ₹1,500 - ₹8,000 Monthly
Pellets (Tin of 500) ₹400 - ₹900 Per Unit
Beginner Air Pistol ₹30,000 - ₹50,000 One-time
Professional Air Rifle ₹2.5 Lakh - ₹4 Lakh One-time

3. Equipment: Rental vs. Buying

During your first six months at a shooting range in Noida, we highly recommend using academy-owned weapons. Once you commit to competitive levels, an imported 10m Air Pistol or Rifle becomes necessary. Brands like Walther and Steyr are the gold standard, often costing upwards of ₹1.8 Lakh.

For official rules and match fees, you can check the [National Rifle Association of India (NRAI)](https://www.thenrai.in) website.
