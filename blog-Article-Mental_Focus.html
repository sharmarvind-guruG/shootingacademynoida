The Zen of the 10m Air Pistol: Master Your Heartbeat

By Coach Sharma Published Feb 15, 2026 Reading Time 5 Mins

In the world of professional shooting, particularly at the 10m Air Pistol Olympic standard, the battle isn't fought on the range—it's fought between your ears. As shooters in Noida prepare for the upcoming State Trials, one question dominates: How do I stay calm?

"Precision is not just about the eye; it is about the stillness of the heart between two beats."

1. The Science of the Trigger Squeeze

Most beginners jerk the trigger. In our Sector 51 sessions, we focus on the 'surprise break'. You shouldn't know exactly when the shot is going off. Your finger should apply steady, increasing pressure until the mechanism releases naturally.

2. Sight Alignment vs. Sight Picture

It is a common mistake to focus on the target. The target should be blurry; your front sight should be crystal clear. Mastering this optical shift is what separates a 9 from a 10.9.
